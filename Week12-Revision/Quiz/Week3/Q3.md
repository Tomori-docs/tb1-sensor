**3. A data acquisition system is designed to measure signals from one photodiode and one piezoelectric sensor. Which of the following combinations of components is most appropriate for this system? ***

A. A charge-to-voltage amplifier for the photodiode, a current-to-voltage amplifier for the piezoelectric sensor, a multiplexer to select between signals, and an analogue-to-digital converter (ADC).

B. A current-to-voltage amplifier for the photodiode, a charge-to-voltage amplifier for the piezoelectric sensor, a multiplexer to select between signals, and an analogue-to-digital converter (ADC).

C. Two voltage followers connected directly to an analogue-to-digital converter (ADC).

D. None of the above.

答案解析：
这道题考察对不同传感器输出信号类型和相应调理电路的理解。

光电二极管 (Photodiode)：通常在反向偏压下工作，其输出信号是电流，与被测光强成正比。因此，需要电流-电压转换放大器 (Transimpedance Amplifier) 将其转换为电压信号。

压电传感器 (Piezoelectric Sensor)：其输出信号是电荷，由机械应力产生。因此，需要电荷-电压转换放大器 (Charge Amplifier) 将其转换为电压信号。
由于有两个传感器，系统需要一个多路复用器 (MUX) 来分时选择不同通道的信号，然后送入一个共用的模数转换器 (ADC) 进行数字化。因此，选项B的组合是正确的。
