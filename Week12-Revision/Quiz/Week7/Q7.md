Describe the difference between reactive ion etching and hydrofluoric
acid (HF) for etching of silicon dioxide. *

Reactive ion etching is anisotropic (different etching rate at different directions) but HF
etching is isotropic (etched at the same rate in all directions).

---

Answer:
Reactive ion etching is anisotropic (different etching rate at different directions) but HF
etching is isotropic (etched at the same rate in all directions).
