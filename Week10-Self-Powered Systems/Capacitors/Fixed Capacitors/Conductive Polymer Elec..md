▪ They use a high-conductive polymer (solid) as an electrolyte
▪ Advantages
– Very low equivalent series resistance (ESR)
– Used as replacements for other types of electrolytic capacitors
▪ Disadvantages
– Expensive
– None of them come with a high rated voltage
