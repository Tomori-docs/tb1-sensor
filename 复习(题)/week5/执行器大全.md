### 记得过等效电路的题目

1. Electrostatic (静电式)	Voltage -> Mechanical movement (电压 -> 机械运动)
![](/file/2/nLYAAnia9vdpnatXjD5UF.png)
---


2. Electromagnetic (电磁式)	Current in coil -> Mechanical movement (线圈中的电流 -> 机械运动)
![](/file/2/LKcIloj20ejoQe-2RS4IM.png)
---
3. Electrodynamic (电动式)	Current in coil -> Mechanical movement (线圈中的电流 -> 机械运动)
![](/file/2/ZVfa5qARhCmSOu3GiRzFr.png)

---
4. Piezoelectric (压电式)	Electric field/voltage -> Mechanical deformation (电场/电压 -> 机械形变)
![](/file/2/f1vmff8A64x-v-Kjs3m7x.png)


---
5. Electrostrictive (电致伸缩式)	Electric field/voltage -> Mechanical deformation (电场/电压 -> 机械形变)
![](/file/2/QvM3oXpZ2R-iGkWlgjo9Y.png)


---
6. Magnetostrictive (磁致伸缩式)	Current in coil -> Mechanical elongation (线圈中的电流 -> 机械伸长)
![](/file/2/3kodEwUBDLhVbiQ3j1FaP.png)


---
7. Thermal (热式)	Voltage -> Thermal expansion -> Mechanical movement (电压 -> 热膨胀 -> 机械运动)
![](/file/2/MMk0u0cwEgDqwgaFKaXir.png)
