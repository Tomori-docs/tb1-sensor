## 传感器类型及其关键效应 (Sensor Types and Key Effects)

### 1. Conductometric (电导式)
Mechanical stress -> Conductivity change (机械应力 -> 电导率变化)
![](/file/2/1bfqb0wqpkiCqLrkaclM5.png)

---

### 2. Capacitive (电容式)
Mechanical stress -> Capacitance change (机械应力 -> 电容变化)
![](/file/2/iQx4bctatqU2i4Ks7lwNM.png)

---

### 3. Piezoelectric (压电式)
Mechanical stress -> Charge change (机械应力 -> 电荷变化)
![](/file/2/jxmu46AJX5FB9E4Xmaoux.png)

---

### 4. Magnetostrictive (磁致伸缩式)
Mechanical change -> Current change (机械变化 -> 电流变化)
![](/file/2/tHQksM7oXuaMXvn7DPsRp.png)
---

### 5. Piezoresistive (压阻式)
Mechanical stress -> Resistance change (机械应力 -> 电阻变化)
![](/file/2/UZYGvEAHoG8W8ty8zYLiC.png)

---

### 6. Optical (光学式)
Changes in light intensity -> Current change (光强度变化 -> 电流变化) 
![](/file/2/DY_dIzC8H9Vb03oxa-rq-.png)

---
### 7. Resonant (谐振式)
Mechanical change -> Resonant frequency change (机械变化 -> 谐振频率变化)
![](/file/2/ddXKv5aYa2kH0pa0Kv-vr.png)

---
### 8. Transistor-based (基于晶体管的)
Mechanical change -> Change in transistor current (机械变化 -> 晶体管电流变化)
![](/file/2/y8SMTRpQMwwfmEmmsfkfB.png)
---
### 9. Acoustic (声学式)
Mechanical change -> Voltage change (机械变化 -> 电压变化)
![](/file/2/cvx8s-gC7OksyxsiJYptu.png)

---
### 10. Temperature (温度式)
Temperature change -> Resistance change (温度变化 -> 电阻变化)
![](/file/2/PhrV0qIhn4dt1EABkdzy-.png)
