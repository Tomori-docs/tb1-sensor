By applying two Vac voltages of V1 and V2
to the fixed plates and biasing the
movable comb at a dc voltage of V0
, the movable comb will move and resonate laterally due
to an alternating electrostatic force.
当两个交流电压V1和V2分别施加于固定梳齿，并使活动梳齿偏置在直流电压V0时，活动梳齿会在交变静电力的作用下横向移动并产生共振。


The equivalent circuit for this actuator includes transformers that represent conversion of electrical
to mechanical energy and vice versa, electrical capacitances (C01, C02) at input and output, a
capacitor representing the total stiffness of two parallel folded beam spring (with a total stiffness of
k), an inductor representing the mass, and a resistance representing the viscous damping.


该驱动器的等效电路包含：实现电能与机械能相互转换的变压器、输入输出端的电容（C01与C02）、代表两根平行折叠梁弹簧总刚度（总刚度系数为k）的电容、代表质量的电感，以及代表粘性阻尼的电阻。

1. Inductor ($L_0$)
：Represents the inherent inductance of the electrical coils or wiring in the actuator,

2. Transducer (The central symbol with $\vec{\psi}$)
：Represents the energy conversion/coupling mechanism (the electrostatic field) itself


3. Capacitor ($1/k$)
Represents the mechanical stiffness (compliance $1/k$) of the system.
