## 传感器类型及其关键效应 (Sensor Types and Key Effects)

### 1. Conductometric (电导式)
Mechanical stress -> Conductivity change (机械应力 -> 电导率变化)
![](/file/2/1bfqb0wqpkiCqLrkaclM5.png)

Principle: Mechanical stress physically alters the material's dimensions or internal structure, which changes its ability to conduct electricity (conductivity). 

原理: 机械应力物理上改变了材料的尺寸或内部结构，从而改变了其导电能力（电导率）。

---

### 2. Capacitive (电容式)
Mechanical stress -> Capacitance change (机械应力 -> 电容变化)
![](/file/2/iQx4bctatqU2i4Ks7lwNM.png)


Principle: An external mechanical stress changes the distance between the two conducting plates or the overlapping area, thereby changing the capacitance ($C = \epsilon A/d$).

原理: 外部机械应力改变了两个导电极板之间的距离或重叠面积，从而改变了电容 ($C = \epsilon A/d$)。

---

### 3. Piezoelectric (压电式)
Mechanical stress -> Charge change (机械应力 -> 电荷变化)
![](/file/2/jxmu46AJX5FB9E4Xmaoux.png)

Mechanical stress -> Charge change (机械应力 -> 电荷变化) Principle: When mechanical stress is applied to certain crystalline materials, it induces an internal polarization, generating an electrical charge proportional to the applied stress. 

原理: 当对某些晶体材料施加机械应力时，会引起内部极化，产生与所施加应力成比例的电荷。

---

### 4. Magnetostrictive (磁致伸缩式)
Mechanical change -> Current change (机械变化 -> 电流变化)
![](/file/2/tHQksM7oXuaMXvn7DPsRp.png)

Principle: Mechanical changes alter the magnetic permeability of the material, which, in a magnetic field, induces a measurable change in current in a coil or circuit. 

原理: 机械变化改变了材料的磁导率，这在磁场中会导致线圈或电路中电流发生可测量的变化。

---

### 5. Piezoresistive (压阻式)
Mechanical stress -> Resistance change (机械应力 -> 电阻变化)
![](/file/2/UZYGvEAHoG8W8ty8zYLiC.png)


Principle: Mechanical stress changes the electrical resistivity of a semiconductor material (like silicon), causing a change in its resistance ($R = \rho L/A$).

原理: 机械应力改变了半导体材料（如硅）的电阻率，从而导致其电阻 ($R = \rho L/A$) 发生变化。

---

### 6. Optical (光学式)
Changes in light intensity -> Current change (光强度变化 -> 电流变化) 
![](/file/2/DY_dIzC8H9Vb03oxa-rq-.png)


Principle: Changes in the intensity of incident light cause a change in the number of charge carriers (electrons and holes) generated within the semiconductor, leading to a measurable change in electric current. 

原理: 入射光强度的变化导致半导体内部产生的载流子（电子和空穴）数量发生变化，从而产生可测量的电流变化。

---
### 7. Resonant (谐振式)
Mechanical change -> Resonant frequency change (机械变化 -> 谐振频率变化)
![](/file/2/ddXKv5aYa2kH0pa0Kv-vr.png)

Principle: A mechanical change (like stress or mass loading) alters the physical stiffness or mass of a micro-structure, which in turn shifts its natural resonant frequency ($f_r \propto \sqrt{k/m}$).

原理: 机械变化（如应力或质量负载）改变了微结构的物理刚度或质量，从而使其固有谐振频率 ($f_r \propto \sqrt{k/m}$) 发生偏移

---
### 8. Transistor-based (基于晶体管的)
Mechanical change -> Change in transistor current (机械变化 -> 晶体管电流变化)
![](/file/2/y8SMTRpQMwwfmEmmsfkfB.png)

Principle: A mechanical change (often stress) alters the physical dimensions or strain on the transistor channel, modulating the electrical field or carrier mobility, and thus changing the transistor's drain current. 

原理: 机械变化（通常是应力）改变了晶体管沟道的物理尺寸或应变，从而调制了电场或载流子迁移率，进而改变了晶体管的漏极电流。

---
### 9. Acoustic (声学式)
Mechanical change -> Voltage change (机械变化 -> 电压变化)
![](/file/2/cvx8s-gC7OksyxsiJYptu.png)

Principle: This sensor utilizes the propagation of mechanical waves (acoustic waves) through a medium. A mechanical change affects the wave propagation characteristics, and the detector converts the received wave change back into a measurable voltage signal. (Note: In the context of a microphone, mechanical vibration of a diaphragm is converted to a voltage signal). 

原理: 这种传感器利用机械波（声波）在介质中的传播。机械变化影响波传播特性，检测器将接收到的波变化转换回可测量的电压信号。（注意：在麦克风的背景下，振膜的机械振动被转换成电压信号）。


---
### 10. Temperature (温度式)
Temperature change -> Resistance change (温度变化 -> 电阻变化)
![](/file/2/PhrV0qIhn4dt1EABkdzy-.png)


Principle: The temperature of the sensing element directly affects the thermal energy of its charge carriers. This energy change modulates the material's electrical conductivity, leading to a predictable and measurable change in its electrical resistance. 

原理: 传感元件的温度直接影响其载流子的热能。这种能量变化调制了材料的电导率，导致其电阻发生可预测和可测量的变化。
