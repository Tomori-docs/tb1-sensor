• Why sensors 1 and 3 need an interface
circuit?

  ➢ Sensors 1 and 3 cannot be directly connected to standard circuits due to their inappropriate output signals (e.g., a strain gauge), so, they require an interface circuit (signal conditioner).  
    传感器1和3由于其输出信号不适用（例如应变片），无法直接连接到标准电路，因此需要接口电路（信号调节器）。
    
---

• Which sensor is active and which one is
passive?

  ➢ Sensors 1, 2, 3, and 5 are passive (i.e., don’t require external power source. e.g., thermocouple) and sensor 4 is active (e.g., a temperature sensitive resistor).  
    传感器1、2、3和5是无源的（即不需要外部电源，例如热电偶），而传感器4是有源的（例如热敏电阻）。
    
---

• Why do we need MUX?


 The main purpose of the MUX in a Data Acquisition System is to allow a single Analog-to-Digital Converter (A/D) to process signals from multiple sensors.
   在数据采集系统中，MUX 的主要目的是允许单个模数转换器 (A/D) 处理来自多个传感器的信号。

• What is the difference between sensors 1 and 5 vs sensors 2, 3, and 4?

## Group1: 2,3,4
Role (角色): Primary Measurement Sensors (主要测量传感器). These are the sensors directly focused on the Object being measured or controlled.

Location (位置): They are placed on or inside the Object itself.

Purpose (目的): Their function is to capture the physical parameters (like stress, temperature, vibration, etc.) that are a direct result of the Actuator's action, or the intrinsic state of the object. They provide the core data about the test specimen.

作用： 主要测量传感器。这些传感器直接关注正在被测量或控制的对象。

位置： 它们位于对象本身上或内部。

目的： 它们的功能是捕获执行器动作所产生的直接结果的物理参数（如应力、温度、振动等），或捕获对象的内在状态。它们提供了关于测试样本的核心数据。

##  Group2: 1
Role (角色): Environmental/External Measurement Sensor (环境/外部测量传感器).

Location (位置): It is located outside of the Object but typically near it.

Purpose (目的): Its role is to measure external conditions (such as ambient temperature, humidity, background noise, or external forces) that might influence the object's behavior or the accuracy of the measurements taken by sensors 2, 3, and 4. This data is often used for compensation or context.

作用： 环境/外部测量传感器。

位置： 它位于对象外部，但通常靠近对象。

目的： 它的作用是测量可能影响对象行为或传感器 2、3 和 4 测量精度的外部条件（例如环境温度、湿度、背景噪声或外部作用力）。这些数据通常用于补偿或提供背景信息。

## Group 3: Sensor 5
第三组：传感器 5
Role (角色): System or Reference Sensor (系统或参考传感器).

Location (位置): It is placed separately and connected to other system components, often the Excitation Circuit or the computer.

Purpose (目的): Sensor 5 is usually used to monitor the health or output of the Data Acquisition System itself, or to provide a crucial timing/reference signal. For example, it might monitor the power supply voltage provided by the Excitation Circuit to ensure its stability, or measure a critical timing pulse for data synchronization. It is less about the object's state and more about the measurement setup's state.

作用： 系统或参考传感器。

位置： 它独立放置，并连接到其他系统组件，通常是激励电路或计算机。

目的： 传感器 5 通常用于监测数据采集系统本身的健康状况或输出，或提供关键的定时/参考信号。例如，它可能会监测由激励电路提供的电源电压以确保其稳定性，或测量用于数据同步的关键时钟脉冲。它关注的不是对象的状态，而是测量设置的状态。

• What is A/D and why do we need it?

It takes a continuous analog electrical signal (usually voltage, like the output from the MUX) and converts it into a discrete digital signal (a binary number).

The Computer (the main controller/processor of the DAS) operates only on digital data. Sensor signals are inherently analog. The A/D converter acts as the crucial link, translating the real-world analog signals into a format the computer can understand, store, process, and display.

作用： 它接收连续的模拟电信号（通常是电压，例如来自 MUX 的输出），并将其转换为离散的数字信号（一个二进制数）。

需要它的原因：

计算机处理 (Computer Processing): 计算机（DAS 的主要控制器/处理器）只处理数字数据。传感器信号本质上是模拟的。A/D 转换器充当关键环节，将真实世界的模拟信号转换成计算机能够理解、存储、处理和显示的格式。

---






  ➢ Sensors are fed into a multiplexer (MUX). A MUX transfers the sensors' data one at a time to an analogue-to-digital converter (A/D or ADC). A/D is required if the sensor produces an analogue signal.  
    传感器信号输入到多路复用器（MUX）。多路复用器将传感器数据逐个传送到模数转换器（A/D或ADC）。如果传感器产生模拟信号，则需要模数转换器。
