

Question 2

The relationship between the dipole moment and the mechanical deformation in a piezoelectric sensor is expressed by: 
$\sigma=cS-eE$ (Eq 1) 
and 
$D=\epsilon_0E+eS$ (Eq 2), 

where 
$\sigma$: Stress,  应力 (力/面积)
$S$: Strain,  应变 (相对形变)
$E$: Electric field, 电场 (电势梯度)
$D$: Electric flux density, 电通量密度
$c$: Elastic stiffness constant, 弹性刚度常数
$e$: Piezoelectric constant, 压电常数
$\epsilon_0$: permittivity of free space. 自由空间介电常数

Describe the dominant phenomena in both equations when $E=0$.


标准答案：When E = 0 in Eq 1, σ = cS defines the Hooke’s law presenting the relation between
stress and strain. If E = 0 in Eq 2, D = eS defines the direct piezoelectric effect where strain
alone can induce polarisation and electrical output.
